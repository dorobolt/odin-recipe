Fried Noodles Recipe

Indonesian Fried Noodles

This tasty noodle dish is the same one my mom used to make for me when I was growing up.
It's definitely comfort food. You can alter it with adding your favorite meats and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 3 (3 ounce) packages ramen noodles (without flavor packets)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt)
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup sweet soy sauce
  • ¼ cup oyster sauce

Direction

  1. Bring a pan of water to a boil, and cook the ramen until tender, about 3 minutes.
  2. Plunge the noodles into cold water to stop the cooking, drain in a colander set in the sink, and drizzle the noodles with 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Set aside.
  3. Place the chicken strips in a bowl, and toss with olive oil, garlic salt, and black pepper.
    Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a wok over high heat.
  4. Stir in the ramen noodles, soy sauce, sweet soy sauce, and oyster sauce, mixing the noodles and sauces
  5. Bring the mixture to a simmer, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
